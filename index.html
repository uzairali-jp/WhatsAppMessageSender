What is Personal Messaging?

Personal messaging allows you to send direct WhatsApp messages to individuals without saving their contact information. Perfect for quick communication and one-time messages.

This type of messaging is ideal for direct and personal communication between two people, whether for business inquiries, customer support, or personal connections.
