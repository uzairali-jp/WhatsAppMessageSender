What is Broadcast Messaging?

Broadcast messaging allows you to send the same WhatsApp message to multiple recipients at once. Perfect for announcements, promotions, updates, or any message you need to share with a group.

How it works: Add phone numbers individually or paste a list, write your message once, and send it to everyone. Each recipient receives the message as an individual chat, maintaining privacy and personal connection.
